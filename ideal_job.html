My Ideal Job

Full Stack Developer

Job description:

The Full Stack Developer will require the individual to take part in business projects and providing support to the Digital team. The day-to-day operations will include the development of Web and native applications and improving DevOps tools. The job will be responsible for developing the front-end and the back-end.

Responsibilities will include implementing business and technology solutions, utilizing SDLC (Software Development Life Cycle) and Agile practices to enhance the assets technology stack, and collaborate amongst your peers and other business areas.


This job appeals to me as it involves working with multiple technologies and different programming languages. The job ensures that it wouldn’t get repetitive and can offer a lot of challenges.

As of late, I have taken an interest in the Software development field as it shows a lot of promise in the IT industry, allows for flexibility to work anywhere in the world and can feel very rewarding building a solution for a business.


Skills, qualifications, and experience that is required:

The skills required for this role will include experience in Software Development, building a portfolio of previous projects. Exposure to cloud technologies such as Azure, Google but preferably AWS. Experience with database query languages (SQL preferred). Programming languages for this will include Python, JavaScript, React, Java, C # or Kotlin. Knowledge with PaaS (platform as a service) environments.

A Bachelor of Information Technology or Computer Science, although not mentioned in the job ad would be advantageous.
